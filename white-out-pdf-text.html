How to White Out Text in a PDF Online

Four steps. No account. Completely free.

01

Open Your PDF File

Drag and drop a PDF onto the upload area or click Select PDF File. Your file loads instantly and is never sent to any server.

02

Match the Background Color

Click Sample Color and click anywhere on the PDF to instantly pick that exact background color — making your whiteout completely invisible.

03

Cover the Text

Drag a rectangle over any text, number, or image you want to hide. Switch to freehand mode for irregular shapes. Navigate pages to redact multiple sections.

04

Download the Redacted PDF

Click Download PDF. All redactions across every page are permanently embedded. Your file downloads instantly — no email, no server.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about whiteout and PDF redaction.

How do I white out text in a PDF for free?
Upload your PDF, optionally use the eyedropper to sample the background color, then drag a rectangle over any text you want to hide. Click Download PDF to save the redacted file instantly.
Are my files uploaded to a server?
No. QwikRedact runs entirely inside your browser. Your files are never transmitted over the internet — making it completely safe for confidential, medical, legal, or financial documents.
How does the color sampler work?
Click Sample Color, then click anywhere on your PDF page. The tool reads the exact pixel color at that point and sets it as your whiteout color, giving you a seamless, invisible cover that blends perfectly with the background.
Can I use black redaction bars instead of white?
Yes. Click the black preset swatch or use the custom color picker to choose any color. Black redaction bars are the standard for official and legal document redaction.
Can I redact multiple pages?
Yes. Use the page navigation arrows to move between pages. Each page's redactions are stored separately and all are embedded together in the final downloaded PDF.
Will this permanently remove the text from the PDF?
The redactions are drawn as opaque rectangles on top of the PDF content, visually covering the text. For sensitive documents requiring permanent data removal at the underlying level, consider professional redaction software alongside this visual cover.
Does this work on phones and tablets?
Yes. QwikRedact works in any modern browser including Safari on iPhone and iPad, and Chrome or Firefox on Android. Touch drawing is fully supported for both rectangle and freehand modes.
