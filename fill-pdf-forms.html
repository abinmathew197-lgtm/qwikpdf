Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about filling PDF forms online for free.

How do I fill a PDF form online without Adobe?
Upload your PDF, select the Text tool, then click on any field to add text. For checkboxes, use the Tick or Cross tools. All elements can be dragged and resized. Click Download Filled PDF when done.
Can I add a tick or checkmark to a PDF checkbox?
Yes. Select the Tick (✓) or Check (✔) tool and click on the checkbox. You can change the color, resize the symbol, and drag it to align perfectly inside the box.
Does this work on flat (non-interactive) PDFs?
Yes. This tool works on any PDF — flat scans, image-based PDFs, and interactive form PDFs. Elements are placed as overlays on top of the page.
Are my files uploaded to a server?
No. Everything happens locally in your browser. Your PDF files are never uploaded to any server, making this safe for confidential documents.
Can I delete an element after placing it?
Yes. Click the × button on any placed element, or use the delete icon in the Elements list on the left. The original PDF is never modified until you click Download.
