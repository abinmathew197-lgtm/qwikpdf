Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about splitting PDF pages online for free.

How do I split a PDF into separate pages online for free?
Upload your PDF using the button above. Every page appears as a numbered thumbnail. Click the Download button on any page to save it as an individual PDF, or click Download All as ZIP to get all pages at once.
Can I download all the split pages as one ZIP file?
Yes. Click the purple Download All as ZIP button in the toolbar. The tool builds every page into a separate PDF, packages them all into a ZIP archive, and downloads it to your device in one step. Each file in the ZIP is named with the source file name and page number.
Can I extract just one specific page from a PDF?
Yes. Each page thumbnail has its own individual Download button. Click it to save just that one page as a standalone single-page PDF. You can download as many individual pages as you need, in any order.
Are my PDF files uploaded to a server when splitting?
No. QwikPDF runs entirely in your browser. Your PDF files never leave your device and are never sent to any external server. This makes the tool safe for confidential, legal, medical, or personal documents.
Can I split multiple PDF files at the same time?
Yes. Upload several PDFs at once and all their pages appear grouped by source file. Download individual pages from any file, or click Download All as ZIP to get every page from all uploaded PDFs in one archive.
Will the split pages lose any quality?
No quality is lost. Each page is copied directly from the source PDF using pdf-lib. Text sharpness, fonts, vector graphics, and images are preserved exactly as they appear in the original. No re-rendering or compression is applied.
