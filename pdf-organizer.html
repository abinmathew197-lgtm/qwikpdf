Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about organizing PDF pages online.

How do I rearrange pages in a PDF online?
Upload your PDF and every page appears as a thumbnail. Drag any thumbnail left or right to change its position in the document. When you're happy with the order, click Download PDF to save the reorganized file.
How do I delete a page from a PDF for free?
Each page thumbnail has a red trash icon below it. Click it to remove that page instantly. The remaining pages close up and renumber automatically. You can delete as many pages as you like before downloading.
Can I rotate a single page without rotating the whole PDF?
Yes. Each thumbnail has a rotate-left and rotate-right button. Clicking either rotates only that specific page by 90 degrees. The rotation is visible in the preview before you download, so you can verify every page looks correct.
Are my PDF files uploaded to a server?
No. QwikPDF processes everything locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your PDF files are never sent over the internet to any server. This makes it safe for confidential, legal, medical, or personal documents.
Can I combine pages from multiple PDF files?
Yes. You can upload multiple PDF files at once and all their pages will appear together in the grid. Each page is labeled with its source file name. Drag pages from any document to any position, then download everything as one single PDF.
Will the quality of my PDF pages be affected?
No quality is lost. Pages are copied directly from the source PDFs and their content — text, fonts, vector graphics, and images — is preserved exactly. Rotation is applied as a page transformation, not re-rendering.
