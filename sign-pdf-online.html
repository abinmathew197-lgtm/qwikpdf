How to Sign a PDF Online for Free

Four simple steps. No account needed. Completely free.

01

Upload Your PDF

Drag and drop one or more PDF files onto the upload area or click Select PDF Files. Every page loads as a live canvas you can click directly on.

02

Create Your Signature

Use the Draw tab to sign with your mouse or finger on a touchscreen. Or use the Upload Image tab to use a PNG or JPG of your existing signature — white backgrounds are removed automatically.

03

Place & Adjust

Click Use This Signature then click anywhere on any page to place it. Drag it to reposition. Use the Size and Opacity sliders to adjust. Click Apply to All Pages to sign the entire document at once.

04

Download the Signed PDF

Click Download Signed PDF. Your signature is permanently embedded as a high-resolution image — clean, full quality, ready to share or print.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about signing PDF documents online for free.

How do I sign a PDF online for free?
Upload your PDF to QwikPDF Sign, draw your signature in the pad or upload a signature image, click Use This Signature, then click anywhere on a page to place it. Drag to reposition, adjust size if needed, and click Download Signed PDF.
Is my PDF uploaded to a server?
No. All processing happens entirely in your browser using pdf-lib and pdf.js. Your files never leave your device, making it completely safe for legal, medical and confidential documents.
Can I draw my signature on a phone or tablet?
Yes. The signature drawing pad fully supports touch input, so you can sign naturally with your finger on a phone or tablet, or with a stylus on a supported device.
How does white background removal work for uploaded images?
When you upload a JPG signature, QwikPDF renders it into a canvas and converts near-white pixels to transparent. This means only your ink appears on the PDF, not a white box around it. PNG files with transparency are used as-is.
Can I add the signature to every page at once?
Yes. Place a signature on one page, click it to select it, then click Apply to All Pages. The signature is duplicated at the same proportional position on every page of that PDF file.
Is a drawn signature legally binding?
In many countries, an electronic signature that represents your intent to sign is legally valid under laws such as eIDAS (EU), ESIGN (US) and similar regulations. However, for high-stakes legal matters, we recommend consulting a legal professional about the requirements in your jurisdiction.
