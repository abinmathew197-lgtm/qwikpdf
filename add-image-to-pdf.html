How to Add an Image to a PDF

Five steps. No account. Completely free.

01

Open Your PDF

Drag and drop any PDF or click Select PDF File. All pages immediately render as thumbnails in the left panel.

02

Select a Page

Click any thumbnail to jump to that page. A teal highlight shows which page is currently active for editing.

03

Insert Your Image

Click Insert Image — only an image picker opens. Choose any JPG, PNG, WebP, or GIF and it appears centered on the active page.

04

Position & Resize

Drag the image anywhere. Pull handles to resize. Drag the teal circle to rotate. Use the properties panel for precise numeric control.

05

Download

Click Download PDF. All images on all pages are permanently embedded at full quality — instant download, no server.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about inserting images into PDFs.

How do I add an image to a specific PDF page?
Open your PDF, click the page thumbnail on the left, then click Insert Image. Your image appears centered on that page, ready to drag and resize.
Are my files uploaded to a server?
No. Everything runs entirely in your browser. Your PDF and image files are never sent to any server.
Can I add images to multiple pages?
Yes. Click any page thumbnail to switch to it and add images independently. All images are embedded in the final PDF.
What image formats are supported?
JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP, and GIF are all supported. Images are embedded at full original quality.
