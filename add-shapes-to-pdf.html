How to Add Icons and Shapes to a PDF Online

Four simple steps. No account. Completely free.

01

Upload Your PDF

Drag and drop one or more PDF files onto the upload area, or click Select PDF Files. Every page loads directly into an interactive canvas that you can click on.

02

Pick an Icon or Shape

Choose from 40+ Google Material Icons organized by category, or pick one of 10 geometric shapes. Set the color, size, and opacity using the controls on the right.

03

Place & Adjust

Click anywhere on a page canvas to place the selected item. Drag it to reposition. Click any existing annotation to select it — then change its color, size or delete it. Use Apply to All Pages to stamp every page.

04

Download the PDF

Click Download Annotated PDF next to any file. All annotations are permanently embedded as images — clean, full quality, instantly downloaded.
