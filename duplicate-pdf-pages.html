How to Duplicate PDF Pages

Three steps. No account. Completely free.

01

Upload Your PDF

Drag and drop your PDF file onto the upload area, or click Select PDF File to browse. All pages are shown as thumbnails instantly.

02

Select & Duplicate Pages

Click any page thumbnail to select it (you can select multiple). Then click Duplicate Selected, or use the Duplicate button on each card to copy that page immediately.

03

Download Your PDF

Reorder pages by dragging if needed, then click Download PDF. Your updated PDF is generated in your browser and saved straight to your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about duplicating PDF pages.

Can I duplicate multiple pages at once?
Yes. Click multiple page thumbnails to select them all, then click Duplicate Selected. Each selected page will be duplicated and inserted right after the original.
Where does the duplicated page appear?
Duplicated pages are inserted immediately after the original page in the page list. You can then drag any page to reorder it before downloading.
Are my files uploaded to a server?
No. Everything runs in your browser using JavaScript and pdf-lib. Your PDF is never sent to any server, making it completely safe for confidential documents.
Will the downloaded PDF have a watermark?
No watermarks are added. The downloaded file is a clean, professional PDF with your original content and the duplicated pages, exactly as shown.
Can I duplicate a page more than once?
Yes. You can duplicate any page as many times as you want — just keep clicking the Duplicate button on the card. Each copy appears after the previous one and can be reordered freely.
Does duplicating reduce PDF quality?
No. Pages are copied exactly from the source PDF with no re-rendering or compression. Text, images, vector graphics, and fonts are all preserved at full original quality.
