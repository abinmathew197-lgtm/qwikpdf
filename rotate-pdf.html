Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about rotating PDF pages online for free.

How do I rotate a PDF page online for free?
Upload your PDF using the button above. Use Rotate ALL pages to fix the whole document at once, or click the ↺/↻ buttons on individual page cards to rotate specific pages. Then click Download Rotated PDF to save the corrected file.
Can I rotate just one page in a PDF without changing the others?
Yes. Each page thumbnail has its own ↺ (rotate left, −90°) and ↻ (rotate right, +90°) buttons. Click them to rotate only that specific page. All other pages remain unchanged. A colored indicator shows which pages have been rotated.
What rotation angles are supported?
You can rotate pages 90° clockwise (+90°), 90° counter-clockwise (−90°), or 180°. Using the global controls you can apply these to all pages at once. On individual pages, click the ↻ button multiple times to add 90° increments (1× = 90°, 2× = 180°, 3× = 270°).
Are my PDF files uploaded to a server when I rotate them?
No. QwikPDF runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your PDF files are never sent to any server — not even temporarily. This makes the tool completely safe for confidential, legal, medical, or personal documents.
Will rotating the PDF reduce its quality or file size?
No quality is lost. Page rotation is applied as a metadata change using pdf-lib — the actual page content (text, fonts, vector graphics, images) is never re-rendered or re-compressed. The output file quality is identical to the original.
Can I rotate multiple PDF files at the same time?
Yes. Upload several PDFs at once and all their pages appear grouped by source file. Apply global rotations to all files, or rotate pages within individual files, then download each corrected PDF separately.
Can I undo a rotation if I make a mistake?
Yes. Click Reset on an individual page card to undo that page's rotation and return it to its original orientation. Use Reset All in the toolbar to undo all rotations across all pages at once.
