How to Crop a PDF

Four steps. No account. Completely free.

01

Open Your PDF

Drag and drop any PDF or click Select PDF File. All pages immediately render as thumbnails in the left panel.

02

Draw the Crop Area

Click and drag directly on the page to draw a crop rectangle. Drag handles to resize, or enter exact pixel values in the panel.

03

Apply the Crop

Click Crop This Page to crop only the current page, or Crop All Pages to apply the same proportional crop to every page in the PDF.

04

Download

Click Download PDF. Your cropped PDF downloads instantly — no server, no watermarks, full quality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about cropping PDFs.

What does "Crop All Pages" do?
It takes the crop region you drew on the current page (as a percentage of the page size) and applies that same proportional region to every page. So if you crop the center 80% of one page, every page will be cropped to its center 80%.
Is my PDF file uploaded anywhere?
No. 100% in-browser processing. Your PDF stays on your device at all times and is never sent to any server.
Can I set different crops for different pages?
Yes. Click each page thumbnail to navigate to it, draw a custom crop area, then click "Crop This Page". Each page can have a completely independent crop. The sidebar shows a list of all pages with crops applied.
Does cropping reduce my file size?
PDF crop uses the PDF mediaBox/cropBox specification to visually crop the page. The underlying content is hidden, not deleted. To get a truly smaller file, use a PDF compressor after cropping.
